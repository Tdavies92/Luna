Night Feeds — What to Keep

At 24 weeks and 7.2kg, 2–3 genuine night feeds are normal and needed. Do not try to drop feeds at the same time as sleep shaping — one thing at a time.

✅ Do feed if it's been 3+ hours since the last feed
🔄 Partner first if it's been under 2.5hrs — try non-feed resettle for 10 mins before you feed
🎯 Goal First stretch of 3–4hrs from bedtime (first feed ~10:30–11pm)
